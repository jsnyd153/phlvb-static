About Us

Philadelphia Volleyball is the hub for adult volleyball in the Philadelphia area.

Our Mission

Philly Volleyball aims to provide an inclusive and welcoming community for volleyball players of all skill levels. We strive to promote the growth and development of the sport of volleyball in Philadelphia by providing opportunities for players to come together, socialize, and enjoy the game in a fun and competitive atmosphere.

We try to create a safe and supportive environment where players can improve their skills, meet new people, and have a great time playing volleyball. We believe that volleyball is more than just a game - it's a community, and we're dedicated to fostering that sense of connection and camaraderie both on and off the court.

Philadelphia Volleyball is a 501c7 organization.

Our Goals:

Fostering Community

We believe that volleyball is more than just a game - it's a community, and we're dedicated to fostering that sense of connection and camaraderie both on and off the court.

Inclusivity

We are committed to creating a welcoming and inclusive community for all players. We welcome players of all backgrounds, ages, and skill levels, and strive to create a safe and supportive environment for everyone.

Healthy Competition

While we are committed to offering opportunities to play at all levels, a big part of the experience for many people is improving and competing. We strive to offer opportunities for high level competition that foster a sense of fair play and comradery.

Growing the Game

Philadelphia is not traditionally known as a big city for volleyball. We’re looking to change that and welcome more people to the game we love.

Our History

In 2014 Dan Elliot started running volleyball events. slowly it blossomed into a large organization, culminating in our official becoming a non profit this past year. throughout it’s existence philly volleyball has been member supported and run by community volunteers/organizers
