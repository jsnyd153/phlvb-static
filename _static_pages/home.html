Welcome to Philly Volleyball

Philly Volleyball is the hub for adult volleyball across the Philadelphia area. Our mission is to provide an inclusive and welcoming community for volleyball players of all skill levels, while promoting the growth and development of the sport in the Philadelphia area. Whether you’re a beginner or a college star, by yourself or have a team, we have ways to play.

How to get started

We use the app called Opensports to organize and publicize events. Events are open to the public, but you must get a ticket on the app to reserve your spot. No need to have a team or a ball, just sign up for an event and bring yourself!
