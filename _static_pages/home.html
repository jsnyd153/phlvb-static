Welcome to Philly Volleyball

Philly Volleyball is the hub for adult volleyball across the Philadelphia area. We strive to provide an inclusive and welcoming community for volleyball players of all skill levels, while promoting the growth and development of the sport in the Philadelphia area. We believe that volleyball is a great way to stay active, meet new people, and have fun. Whether you’re a beginner or a college star, by yourself or with a team, we have ways to play.

How to get started

We use the app Opensports to organize and publicize events. Events are open to the public, but you must get a ticket on the app to reserve your spot. No need to have a team or a ball, just sign up for an event and bring yourself!
