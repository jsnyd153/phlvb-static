Leagues

The Philadelphia Volleyball Leagues are a great way to get in shape and meet new people. Our leagues run on a quarterly basis throughout the year. We currently offer two levels of adult co-ed indoor volleyball played at North Light Community Center. Make a team and play every week, then grab a beer at our sponsor bar down the street.
