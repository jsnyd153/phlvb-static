We use the app called opensports to organize and publicize events. Events are open to the public, but you must have a reservation through the app to attend.

  • Download the Opensports app

  • Create an account

  • Join the Philadelphia Volleyball group

  • Add a payment method

  • Select and join an event

Recommended: set up your sports cards (Indoor volleyball, grass volleyball, sand volleyball). Most hosts use sports cards to invite people to events
